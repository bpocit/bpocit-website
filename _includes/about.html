About


The purpose of our BPoCiT study group is to offer a safer space for beginners with no to little knowledge of computer science, web development and web design. This study group aims to empower and motivate Queer_ Trans_ Inter_ Black & People of Color/Black and People of Color (QTIBPOC/BPOC) to get into coding, having fun time together and to ask any questions without fear and rush.

Our general concept is to meet, hang out together and to learn from another in a relaxed environment - each at their own interest, pace and speed.

The initial group is located in Berlin and meets twice a month. From time to time we will offer workshops on specific topics either facilitated by us or external guests.

To ensure an effective learning environment, our hosting spaces are limited which is why we ask you to RSVP for each session via our Events Calendar.
