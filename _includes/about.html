About


We are a group of Black and People of Color in Tech (BPoCiT) that meets in Berlin to learn coding together. Some of us are friends and friends of our friends. At the moment we are taking a break due to lack of time but will continue with our project group as soon as our capacities allow.

The purpose of our BPoCiT study group is to offer a safer space for beginners with no to little knowledge of computer science, web development and web design. This study group aims to empower and motivate Queer_ Trans_ Inter_ Black & People of Color/Black and People of Color (QTIBPOC/BPOC) to get into coding, having fun time together and to ask any questions without fear and rush. People that are descended from mixed backgrounds are very much welcome to join our group as well.

Our general concept is to meet, hang out together and to learn from another in a relaxed environment - each at their own interest, pace and speed. From time to time we will offer workshops on specific topics either facilitated by us or external guests.

To ensure an effective learning environment, our hosting spaces are limited to max. 12 people for which we ask you to register via our contact page. Please find all dates in our events calendar.
